Our Story

Brett Graham's
vision on a plate

Tucked quietly on Ledbury Road in the heart of Notting Hill, The Ledbury opened in 2005 and has become one of London's most celebrated restaurants. Chef-patron Brett Graham rears his own deer, Jersey cattle and Iberian pigs, and grows mushrooms in the restaurant's own in-house Fungarium — produce as personal as the cooking itself.

After a two-year hiatus, The Ledbury reopened with a stunning new dining room and was awarded three Michelin stars in the 2024 guide — a rare honour shared by only a handful of restaurants across the UK. The kitchen is led by Head Chef Tom Spenceley, the dining room by General Manager Jack Settle.

The Fungarium

Unique to The Ledbury — an in-house mushroom growing cabinet where rare and seasonal fungi are cultivated and served at their absolute peak. A living ingredient, steps from the kitchen.

Own-Reared Produce

Brett Graham rears deer, Jersey cattle and Iberian pigs himself. The menu is rooted in produce he has invested in personally — traceability taken to its most intimate conclusion.

800-Bin Wine Cellar

Built over twenty years, the cellar spans classic European terroirs with rare old Burgundy offered at prices that reward true wine lovers. Paired thoughtfully by our sommelier team.

What We Offer

Beyond the table

01
Private Dining

Our private dining space seats up to 14 guests for an exclusive, fully bespoke experience. Whether it's a birthday, anniversary, or corporate evening — we will tailor every detail, from the menu to the wine list, to suit your occasion perfectly.

02
Gift Cards

The gift of an exceptional meal. Our gift cards are available in any denomination and can be used toward dinner, lunch, or private events. The perfect present for someone who appreciates the finer things.

03
Wine Pairing

Let our sommelier guide you through an expertly curated wine journey alongside your meal. Each glass is chosen to elevate — never overshadow — the food on your plate. A classic pairing or something a little unexpected? We can do both.

04
Special Occasions

From proposals to milestone birthdays, we love being part of life's important moments. Tell us what you're celebrating and we'll make sure the evening reflects exactly that — quietly, tastefully, and without fuss.
